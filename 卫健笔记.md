## 通用SQL where

```sql
-- oracle --
where EFFECTIVETIME_P 
	between TO_DATE(${beginTime},'YYYY-MM-DD HH24:MI:SS') 
	and TO_DATE(${endTime},'YYYY-MM-DD HH24:MI:SS')

-- sqlserver --
where EFFECTIVETIME_P 
	between CONVERT(VARCHAR(19), ${beginTime}, 101) 
	and CONVERT(VARCHAR(19), ${endTime}, 101)
```
